     Space is everything in the universe beyond the top of the Earth’s atmosphere – the Moon, where the GPS satellites orbit, Mars, other stars, the Milky Way, black holes, and distant quasars. Space also means what’s between planets, moons, stars, etc – it’s the near-vacuum otherwise known as the interplanetary medium, the interstellar medium, the inter-galactic medium, the intra-cluster medium, etc.

Astronomy is the science that studies the laws of the stars, celestial objects and phenomena.

Cosmology is a branch of physics and metaphysics dealing with the nature of the universe.
